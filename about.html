Our Founder

Professional Experience & Passion

18×26 NexGen is a private individual and group soccer training program dedicated to developing the next generation of elite players. Our program communicates a professional, performance-driven brand that connects with athletes and families seeking high-quality technical and tactical training.

  • Played in MLS NEXT Pro for Columbus Crew 2 (2022 & 2024), drafted by NYCFC in 2019
  • USL career with Memphis 901, New Mexico United, Greenville Triumph, and Oakland Roots
  • Represented Somalia at the national team level for 5 years
  • Scouting experience with Columbus Crew as a North American scout
  • Training philosophy focused on technical skill, tactical awareness, and mentoring players
